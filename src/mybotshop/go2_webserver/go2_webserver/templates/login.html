Safety Notice

Authorized Personnel Only

Important
  • Operated by authorized trained personnel only.
  • Follow all safety protocols and guidelines.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and potential hazards.
Pre-Operation Checklist
  • Ensure the robot is powered on and connected to the network.
  • Check that all required services are running.
  • Verify that the robot's sensors are functioning properly.
  • Ensure the robot is in a safe environment for operation.
  • Confirm that the robot's battery is charged and ready for use.
  • Ensure the robot's navigation system is calibrated.
Safety Requirements
  • Ensure the emergency stop button is accessible and functional.
  • Ensure the communication systems are operational.
  • Check that the robot's cameras and sensors are clean and unobstructed.
  • Confirm that the payload is securely attached and balanced.
  • Ensure the wheels or tracks are free from debris.
  • Review all safety protocols and emergency procedures.
  • Ensure the path is clear of obstacles and hazards.
  • Verify that sensors are calibrated for the environment.
  • Review system logs to ensure there are no errors.
  • Ensure stable communication with the control system.
